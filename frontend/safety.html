Home Safety Tips

Essential safety measures to protect your home and family from climate-related disasters

High Priority
Floods

Flood Safety Measures

Essential tips to protect your home and family during flooding

  • Install flood barriers and sandbags around entry points
  • Keep emergency supplies on higher floors
  • Ensure drainage systems are clear and functional
  • Secure your home by moving valuables to higher floors and sealing doors and windows
  • Check that your home insurance covers flood damage
Medium Priority
Heatwaves

Heatwave Protection

Keep your home cool and safe during extreme heat events

  • Install reflective window films and proper insulation
  • Use fans and air conditioning efficiently
  • Create cool zones with adequate ventilation
  • Ensure adequate ventilation and avoid using heat-generating appliances during peak hours
  • Limit outdoor activities, especially during peak heat hours
High Priority
Landslides

Landslide Preparedness

Protect your property from slope failures and ground movement

  • Install proper drainage to redirect water away from slopes
  • Plant vegetation to stabilize soil
  • Monitor for warning signs like cracks or tilting
  • Monitor local news and disaster alerts continuously
  • Avoid building or staying near steep slopes, riverbanks, or unstable terrain
